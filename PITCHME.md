#### Water flow trought the root cylinder

Three different pathways:

- Apoplastic pathway
	- mass movement
	- water potential differences
	- high transpiration (day)

- Symplatic pathway
	- diffusion
	- osmotic differences 
	- low transpiration (night)

- Transcellular pathway
